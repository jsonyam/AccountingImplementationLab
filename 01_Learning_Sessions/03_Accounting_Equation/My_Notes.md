\# Lesson 03 – My Notes



\## Summary



The Accounting Equation is the foundation of accounting.



Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity



Every accounting transaction affects at least two accounts while keeping the equation balanced.



This principle applies regardless of the accounting software being used.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Assets represent what the business owns.

\- Liabilities represent what the business owes.

\- Owner's Equity represents the owner's interest in the business.

\- Every transaction keeps the equation balanced.

\- ERP and accounting systems are built around this principle.
