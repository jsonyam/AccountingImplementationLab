\# Lesson 03 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is the Accounting Equation?



Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity.



\---



\## 2. Why is it important?



Because every accounting transaction must keep the business financially balanced.



\---



\## 3. What happens if the equation doesn't balance?



The accounting records are incorrect and financial statements become unreliable.



\---



\## 4. Does QuickBooks use the Accounting Equation?



Yes.



Every invoice, bill, payment, and journal entry updates the Accounting Equation automatically.



\---



\## 5. Does Shopify use the Accounting Equation?



Not directly.



Shopify creates business transactions.



Accounting software records their financial impact.
