\# Lesson 03 – ERP Connections



\## Objective



Understand how ERP systems apply the Accounting Equation.



\---



\## Business Flow



Purchase Order



↓



Receive Inventory



↓



Inventory Updated



↓



Supplier Invoice



↓



Accounting Entry



↓



Financial Statements



\---



Every ERP transaction eventually updates:



\- Assets

\- Liabilities

\- Owner's Equity



QuickBooks, Xero, SAP, Oracle, and Odoo all enforce this principle.



The screens may look different.



The accounting never changes.



\---



\## Consultant Perspective



Implementation Consultants don't memorize screens.



They understand how business events affect financial data.



That knowledge transfers between accounting systems.
