\# Lesson 03 – Accounting Equation



\# Why This Matters



If you understand the Accounting Equation, every accounting transaction will make sense.



Every accounting system—from QuickBooks and Xero to SAP and Oracle—follows this rule.



\---



\# The Accounting Equation



Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity



\---



\# What is an Asset?



Assets are resources the business owns.



Examples:



\- Cash

\- Bank Account

\- Inventory

\- Equipment

\- Computer

\- Accounts Receivable



Question:



"What does the business own?"



\---



\# What is a Liability?



Money the business owes.



Examples:



\- Loans

\- Credit Cards

\- Supplier Bills

\- Taxes Payable



Question:



"Who do we owe?"



\---



\# What is Owner's Equity?



The owner's share of the business.



Examples:



\- Capital Investment

\- Retained Earnings

\- Profit



Question:



"What belongs to the owner?"



\---



\# Real Example



You invest ₱100,000.



Assets



Cash = ₱100,000



Liabilities = ₱0



Owner's Equity = ₱100,000



Equation:



100,000 = 0 + 100,000



Balanced.



\---



You buy inventory worth ₱20,000 using cash.



Assets



Cash ↓ 20,000



Inventory ↑ 20,000



Assets changed.



Total Assets remain ₱100,000.



Equation still balances.



\---



You borrow ₱50,000 from the bank.



Assets



Cash ↑ ₱50,000



Liabilities



Loan ↑ ₱50,000



Equation



150,000 = 50,000 + 100,000



Balanced.



\---



\# ERP Perspective



ERP records business events.



Accounting records their financial impact.



Every transaction must keep the Accounting Equation balanced.



If it doesn't...



...the system rejects it.



\---



\# QuickBooks



Every invoice...



Every bill...



Every payment...



Every journal entry...



changes the Accounting Equation.



The software simply automates it.



\---



\# Xero



Exactly the same principle.



Different interface.



Same accounting.



\---



\# Shopify



Shopify creates business events.



QuickBooks records the accounting impact.



\---



\# Key Takeaways



Assets



=



Liabilities



\+



Owner's Equity



Never changes.



Everything in accounting follows this equation.
