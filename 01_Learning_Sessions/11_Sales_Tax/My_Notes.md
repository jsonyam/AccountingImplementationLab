\# Lesson 11 – My Notes



\## Summary



Sales Tax is collected from customers and temporarily held by the business before being paid to the government.



It is recorded as a liability rather than revenue.



QuickBooks, Xero, ERP systems, and Shopify automate tax calculation when configured correctly.



Implementation Consultants configure tax settings to ensure compliance and accurate financial reporting.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Sales Tax is not business income.

\- Sales Tax creates a liability.

\- Tax configuration affects invoices and reports.

\- Accounting systems automate tax calculations.

\- Consultants configure tax rules during implementation.
