\# Lesson 11 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is Sales Tax?



A tax collected from customers on behalf of the government.



\---



\## 2. Is Sales Tax business income?



No.



It is a liability until remitted to the government.



\---



\## 3. Why is Sales Tax configuration important?



Incorrect configuration results in incorrect invoices, tax reporting, and financial statements.



\---



\## 4. What systems calculate Sales Tax?



QuickBooks, Xero, ERP systems, and Shopify can all calculate Sales Tax when properly configured.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand Sales Tax?



Because tax configuration is one of the most important parts of any accounting implementation.
