\# Lesson 08 – Interview Questions



\## 1. Why are bank accounts important in accounting?



They record the movement of business cash and support accurate financial reporting.



\---



\## 2. What is a bank feed?



A bank feed automatically imports transactions from the bank into accounting software.



\---



\## 3. What is a bank transfer?



A movement of money between business bank accounts.



\---



\## 4. Does a bank transfer create income?



No.



It simply moves cash between accounts.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand banking?



Because bank configuration, payment processing, and reconciliation are essential parts of an accounting implementation.
