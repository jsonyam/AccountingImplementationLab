\# Lesson 08 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Understand how banking transactions affect accounting records.



\---



\## Business Scenario



Today the following transactions occurred.



1\. Customer paid ₱15,000.

2\. Paid supplier ₱8,000.

3\. Paid rent ₱5,000.

4\. Owner deposited ₱50,000.

5\. Transferred ₱20,000 to savings.



\---



\## Exercise 1



Which transactions increased the bank balance?



\---



\## Exercise 2



Which transactions decreased the bank balance?



\---



\## Exercise 3



Which transaction is neither income nor expense?



\---



\## Reflection



Why should every business separate personal and business bank accounts?
