\# Lesson 08 – Banking



\# Learning Objectives



By the end of this lesson, I should be able to:



\- Understand the role of bank accounts in accounting.

\- Explain how business bank transactions affect financial records.

\- Understand deposits, withdrawals, transfers, and bank feeds.

\- Explain how QuickBooks and Xero manage banking.

\- Understand the consultant's role in banking configuration.



\---



\# Why Banking Matters



Every business handles money through bank accounts.



Almost every financial transaction eventually affects the bank.



Examples include:



\- Customer payments

\- Supplier payments

\- Payroll

\- Taxes

\- Operating expenses

\- Loan payments



\---



\# Common Banking Transactions



Money In



\- Customer Payments

\- Owner Investment

\- Loan Proceeds

\- Refunds Received



Money Out



\- Supplier Payments

\- Rent

\- Utilities

\- Salaries

\- Advertising

\- Taxes



\---



\# Types of Business Bank Accounts



Checking Account



Used for daily operations.



Savings Account



Used for reserve funds.



Credit Card Account



Used for business purchases.



Loan Account



Tracks borrowed money.

\# Real Example



Customer pays ₱12,000.



Bank Account



+₱12,000



Accounts Receivable



\-₱12,000



Cash increases.



Receivable disappears.



\---



\# Bank Transfers



Money can move between business bank accounts.



Example:



Checking → Savings



No income.



No expense.



Only cash moves between accounts.
