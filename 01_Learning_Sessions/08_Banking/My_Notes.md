\# Lesson 08 – My Notes



\## Summary



Banking records the movement of business cash.



Customer payments, supplier payments, expenses, and deposits all affect bank accounts.



QuickBooks and Xero provide bank feeds, transaction matching, and banking automation.



Implementation Consultants configure bank accounts and payment workflows during system implementation.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Business bank accounts track cash movement.

\- Bank feeds automate transaction imports.

\- Transfers are not income or expenses.

\- Banking supports accurate financial reporting.

\- Consultants configure banking during implementation.
