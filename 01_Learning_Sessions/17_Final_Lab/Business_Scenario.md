\# Lesson 17 – Final Lab



\# Business Scenario



\## Client



LuxeScents Perfume Co.



LuxeScents is a growing ecommerce company selling premium perfumes through Shopify.



The company has hired you as an Implementation Consultant to implement QuickBooks Online and prepare the business for future growth.



\---



\## Business Requirements



The client needs to:



\- Configure the Chart of Accounts

\- Import Products

\- Import Customers

\- Create Suppliers

\- Configure Sales Tax

\- Connect Business Bank Accounts

\- Track Inventory

\- Record Sales

\- Record Supplier Bills

\- Reconcile Bank Accounts

\- Generate Financial Statements



\---



\## Project Goal



Deliver a working accounting system that accurately reflects the company's financial activity and supports management reporting.



\---



\## Consultant Responsibilities



\- Discovery

\- Requirement Gathering

\- Solution Design

\- System Configuration

\- Data Migration

\- User Acceptance Testing (UAT)

\- User Training

\- Go-Live Support

\- Hypercare
