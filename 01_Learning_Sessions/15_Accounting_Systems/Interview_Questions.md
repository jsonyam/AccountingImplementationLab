\# Lesson 15 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is an accounting system?



Software that records and reports financial transactions.



\---



\## 2. What is the difference between QuickBooks and an ERP?



QuickBooks focuses on accounting.



ERP manages accounting plus business operations.



\---



\## 3. Is Shopify an accounting system?



No.



Shopify manages ecommerce while accounting systems manage financial records.



\---



\## 4. Why do businesses integrate accounting systems?



To eliminate duplicate work, improve reporting, and automate processes.



\---



\## 5. What does an Implementation Consultant do?



They configure systems, migrate data, test processes, train users, and support successful implementation.
