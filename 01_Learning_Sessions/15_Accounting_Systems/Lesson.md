\# What is an Accounting System?



An Accounting System is software that records, processes, stores, and reports financial transactions.



Its purpose is to automate bookkeeping while providing accurate financial information.



\---



\# Why Businesses Use Accounting Systems



Businesses use accounting systems to:



\- Record transactions

\- Manage customers

\- Manage suppliers

\- Track inventory

\- Monitor cash flow

\- Generate reports

\- Support tax compliance



\---



\# Common Features



\- General Ledger

\- Accounts Receivable

\- Accounts Payable

\- Banking

\- Inventory

\- Sales Tax

\- Financial Statements

\- Reporting



\---



\# Types of Accounting Systems



\## QuickBooks



Best for:



\- Small businesses

\- Service companies

\- Retail businesses



\---



\## Xero



Best for:



\- Small to medium businesses

\- Cloud accounting

\- Multi-user collaboration



\---



\## ERP Systems



Examples:



\- SAP

\- Oracle NetSuite

\- Microsoft Dynamics

\- Odoo



ERP systems combine accounting with operations.



\---



\## Shopify



Shopify is not an accounting system.



It manages:



\- Products

\- Orders

\- Customers

\- Payments



Accounting is handled by QuickBooks, Xero, or an ERP.
