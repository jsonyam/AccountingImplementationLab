\# Lesson 16 – Shopify Accounting Integration



\# Learning Objectives



By the end of this lesson, I should be able to:



\- Understand why Shopify integrates with accounting systems.

\- Explain the flow of financial data from Shopify to QuickBooks or Xero.

\- Identify common integration points.

\- Understand common implementation challenges.

\- Explain the consultant's role during integration.



\---



\# Why Integrate Shopify?



Shopify manages ecommerce operations.



Accounting systems manage financial records.



Integrating them eliminates duplicate data entry and improves reporting accuracy.
