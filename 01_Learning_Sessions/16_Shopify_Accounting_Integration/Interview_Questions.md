\# Lesson 16 – Interview Questions



\## 1. Why integrate Shopify with QuickBooks or Xero?



To automate accounting, reduce manual work, and improve reporting.



\---



\## 2. What information is commonly synchronized?



Products, customers, orders, payments, inventory, taxes, and refunds.



\---



\## 3. What are common implementation challenges?



Duplicate records, incorrect mapping, tax issues, inventory mismatches, and payment timing differences.



\---



\## 4. What is data mapping?



The process of matching information between two systems so data is recorded correctly.



\---



\## 5. What does an Implementation Consultant do during integration?



They gather requirements, configure the integration, test data flow, validate results, and support go-live.
