\# Lesson 16 – My Notes



\## Summary



Shopify and accounting systems work together to automate financial processes.



Shopify manages ecommerce while QuickBooks or Xero manages accounting.



Successful integrations require accurate mapping, testing, and monitoring.



Implementation Consultants ensure data flows correctly between systems.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Shopify is not an accounting system.

\- Integration reduces manual work.

\- Data mapping is critical.

\- Testing prevents reporting errors.

\- Consultants validate the complete integration.
