\# Lesson 02 – Interview Questions



\## Basic Questions



\### 1. What is US GAAP?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



US GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles) is a standardized set of accounting rules used in the United States to ensure financial information is recorded and reported consistently.



\---



\### 2. Why is US GAAP important?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



US GAAP provides consistency, accuracy, and comparability in financial reporting. It allows investors, management, and regulators to evaluate companies using the same accounting standards.



\---



\### 3. What is the difference between US GAAP and IFRS?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



US GAAP is primarily used in the United States and is developed by the Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB). IFRS is used in many other countries and is developed by the International Accounting Standards Board (IASB).



\---



\### 4. Does QuickBooks create accounting rules?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



No. QuickBooks is accounting software. It records and reports transactions, but the accounting rules come from the accounting standards and the company's accounting policies.



\---



\### 5. Is Shopify an accounting system?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



No. Shopify is an ecommerce platform that manages products, orders, customers, and payments. Businesses typically integrate Shopify with accounting software such as QuickBooks or Xero for financial reporting.



\---



\## Consultant Question



\### As an Implementation Consultant, why should you understand US GAAP?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



Understanding US GAAP helps ensure that system configurations and business processes support accurate financial reporting. While accountants define the accounting policies, consultants need to understand how business transactions flow into the accounting system and affect financial statements.
