\# Lesson 02 – My Notes



\## Summary



US GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles) provides the accounting rules that businesses in the United States use to prepare financial statements.



Accounting software such as QuickBooks and Xero helps businesses apply these rules, but the software itself does not create accounting standards.



ERP systems manage business operations, while accounting systems convert those operational activities into financial information.



Shopify manages ecommerce transactions, which are later recorded in the accounting system.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- US GAAP is an accounting standard.

\- Accounting standards create consistency.

\- QuickBooks and Xero support accounting but do not define accounting rules.

\- ERP and accounting systems have different responsibilities.

\- Business transactions eventually become financial transactions.



\---



\## Real-World Connection



When implementing an accounting system, I need to understand:



\- The client's business process.

\- The accounting standard they follow.

\- How operational transactions flow into financial reporting.

\- How Shopify, ERP, and accounting software integrate.
