\# Lesson 02 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Understand how accounting standards guide the recording of business transactions.



\---



\# Business Scenario



You own a premium perfume store that follows US GAAP.



Today the following transactions occurred:



1\. Invested ₱100,000 into the business.

2\. Purchased ₱25,000 of inventory from a supplier.

3\. Sold ₱8,000 worth of perfume.

4\. Paid ₱2,500 for Facebook advertising.

5\. Customer paid immediately using a credit card.



\---



\# Exercise 1



Which transactions should be recorded in the accounting system?



\- Capital Investment

\- Inventory Purchase

\- Sales

\- Advertising Expense

\- Customer Payment
