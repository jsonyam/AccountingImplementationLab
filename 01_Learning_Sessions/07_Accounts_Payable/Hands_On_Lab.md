\# Lesson 07 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Understand how supplier bills become Accounts Payable.



\---



\## Business Scenario



You purchase inventory worth ₱60,000.



Supplier gives you:



Payment Terms = Net 30



\---



\## Exercise 1

Which account increases?



Which account does not change?



\---



\## Exercise 2



Thirty days later you pay the supplier.



Which account decreases?



Which liability disappears?



\---



\## Exercise 3



Why is Accounts Payable considered a Liability?
