\# Lesson 07 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is Accounts Payable?



Money a business owes suppliers for goods or services already received.



\---



\## 2. Why is AP a Liability?



Because it represents a future payment obligation.



\---



\## 3. What reduces Accounts Payable?



Paying the supplier.



\---



\## 4. What report monitors unpaid supplier bills?



Accounts Payable Aging Report.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand AP?



Because purchasing, supplier management, payment terms, approvals, and cash flow all depend on proper AP configuration.
