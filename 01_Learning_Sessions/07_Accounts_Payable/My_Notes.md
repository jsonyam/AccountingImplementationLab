\# Lesson 07 – My Notes



\## Summary



Accounts Payable tracks money owed to suppliers.



Recording a supplier bill increases AP.



Paying the supplier reduces AP.



QuickBooks, Xero, and ERP systems automate the AP process while maintaining accurate financial records.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- AP is a Liability.

\- Supplier bills create AP.

\- Supplier payments reduce AP.

\- AP Aging Reports monitor unpaid supplier bills.

\- Consultants configure the AP process during implementation.
