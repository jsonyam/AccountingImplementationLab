\# Lesson 07 – Accounts Payable (AP)



\# Learning Objectives



By the end of this lesson, I should be able to:



\- Understand what Accounts Payable (AP) is.

\- Explain why AP is a liability.

\- Understand the Accounts Payable lifecycle.

\- Explain how QuickBooks and Xero manage supplier payments.

\- Understand the consultant's role in configuring AP.



\---



\# What is Accounts Payable?



Accounts Payable (AP) is money the business owes suppliers for goods or services already received.



The business has received value but has not yet paid cash.



\---



\# Why is AP a Liability?



Because it represents an obligation.



The business must pay the supplier in the future.



\---



\# Accounts Payable Lifecycle



Supplier



↓



Purchase Order (Optional)



↓



Receive Goods



↓



Supplier Invoice (Bill)



↓



Accounts Payable



↓



Supplier Payment



↓



Bill Closed



\---



\# Real Example



You purchase ₱40,000 worth of perfume inventory from your supplier.



Payment Terms:



Net 30



Today



Inventory increases.



Accounts Payable increases.



Cash does not change.



Thirty days later



You pay the supplier.



Cash decreases.



Accounts Payable decreases.

\# Shopify
