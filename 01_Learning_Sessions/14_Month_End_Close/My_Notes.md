\# Lesson 14 – My Notes



\## Summary



Month-End Close ensures that all accounting records are complete before financial reports are generated.



It combines reconciliation, adjustments, journal entries, and reporting into one structured process.



QuickBooks, Xero, and ERP systems all provide tools to support the closing process.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Month-End Close improves financial accuracy.

\- Reconciliation is part of the closing process.

\- Financial statements should only be generated after review.

\- ERP systems support structured closing workflows.

\- Consultants configure closing procedures.
