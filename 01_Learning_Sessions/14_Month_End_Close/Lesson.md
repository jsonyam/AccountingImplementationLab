\# What is Month-End Close?



Month-End Close is the process of verifying and completing all accounting activities for a month before financial reports are finalized.



Its purpose is to ensure financial statements are complete and accurate.



\---



\# Why is Month-End Close Important?



It helps businesses:



\- Produce accurate financial reports

\- Detect accounting errors

\- Reconcile balances

\- Prepare tax reports

\- Support management decisions



\---



\# Typical Month-End Close Checklist



\- Record all sales

\- Record all expenses

\- Review Accounts Receivable

\- Review Accounts Payable

\- Reconcile bank accounts

\- Record depreciation

\- Record accruals

\- Review inventory

\- Review journal entries

\- Generate financial statements
