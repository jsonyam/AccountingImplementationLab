\# Lesson 14 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Practice a basic Month-End Close.



\---



\## Business Scenario



Before closing the month, you discover:



\- One unpaid supplier bill

\- One unreconciled bank transaction

\- Missing internet expense

\- Inventory count differs by 3 units



\---



\## Exercise 1



List the items that should be completed before closing the month.



\---



\## Exercise 2



Why should bank reconciliation be completed before generating financial statements?



\---



\## Exercise 3



Why should inventory be reviewed before closing?



\---



\## Reflection



What problems might occur if the month is closed with missing transactions?
