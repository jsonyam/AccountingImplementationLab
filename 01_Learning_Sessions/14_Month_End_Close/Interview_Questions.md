\# Lesson 14 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is Month-End Close?



The process of completing and reviewing accounting records before finalizing financial reports.



\---



\## 2. Why is Month-End Close important?



It ensures financial statements are accurate and complete.



\---



\## 3. What activities are normally included?



\- Bank Reconciliation

\- Journal Entries

\- Inventory Review

\- AR Review

\- AP Review

\- Financial Reporting



\---



\## 4. What happens after Month-End Close?



Financial statements are reviewed by management and used for decision-making.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand Month-End Close?



Because accounting systems must support an efficient and accurate closing process.
