\# Lesson 12 – My Notes



\## Summary



Financial statements summarize the financial health of a business.



Every accounting transaction contributes to one or more reports.



QuickBooks, Xero, and ERP systems automatically generate financial statements from recorded transactions.



Implementation Consultants ensure that configuration produces accurate and reliable reports.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Financial statements support business decisions.

\- The Balance Sheet shows financial position.

\- The Income Statement shows profitability.

\- The Cash Flow Statement shows cash movement.

\- Accurate configuration leads to accurate reports.



\---

\- Which report shows profit?

\- Which report shows assets and liabilities?

\- Why are financial statements important?

\- How do ERP systems generate financial statements?
