\# Lesson 12 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What are financial statements?



Reports that summarize a company's financial performance and financial position.



\---



\## 2. What are the three primary financial statements?



\- Balance Sheet

\- Income Statement

\- Cash Flow Statement



\---



\## 3. Which report shows profit?



Income Statement.



\---



\## 4. Which report shows Assets, Liabilities, and Equity?



Balance Sheet.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand financial statements?



Because clients evaluate the success of an implementation through accurate financial reporting.
