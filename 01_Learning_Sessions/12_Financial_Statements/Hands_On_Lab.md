\# Lesson 12 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Understand which financial statement is affected by common business transactions.



\---



\## Business Scenario



Today's activities:



\- Customer purchased ₱10,000 of products.

\- Paid ₱2,000 rent.

\- Purchased ₱5,000 inventory.

\- Borrowed ₱20,000 from the bank.



\---



\## Exercise 1



Which transactions affect the Balance Sheet?



\---



\## Exercise 2



Which transactions affect the Income Statement?



\---



\## Exercise 3



Which transactions affect Cash Flow?
