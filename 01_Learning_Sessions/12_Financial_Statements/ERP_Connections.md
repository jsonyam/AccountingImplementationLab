\# Lesson 12 – ERP Connections



\## Objective



Understand how ERP systems produce financial statements.



\---



Business Operations



↓



Accounting Entries



↓



General Ledger



↓



Financial Statements



\---



ERP modules feed accounting automatically.



Purchasing



↓



Inventory



↓



Sales



↓



Payroll



↓



General Ledger



↓



Reports



\---



Implementation Consultants validate:



\- Account Mapping

\- Posting Rules

\- Financial Reports



\---



\## Key Takeaway



Financial statements are the final output of all business transactions.
