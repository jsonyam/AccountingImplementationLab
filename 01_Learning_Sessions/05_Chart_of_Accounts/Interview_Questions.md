\# Lesson 05 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is a Chart of Accounts?



A Chart of Accounts is the complete list of accounts used by a business to record financial transactions.



\---



\## 2. Why is it important?



It organizes financial data and ensures transactions are recorded consistently.



\---



\## 3. Who designs the Chart of Accounts?



Typically the business, accountants, and implementation consultants work together to design or customize it.



\---



\## 4. Does every business have the same Chart of Accounts?



No.



Each business customizes its Chart of Accounts based on its industry and reporting requirements.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand the Chart of Accounts?



Because every accounting transaction depends on proper account mapping.
