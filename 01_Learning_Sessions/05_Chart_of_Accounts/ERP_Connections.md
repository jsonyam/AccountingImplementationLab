\# Lesson 05 – ERP Connections



\## Objective



Understand how ERP systems use the Chart of Accounts.



\---



Business Event



↓



Sales Order



↓



Invoice



↓



Accounting Entry



↓



Chart of Accounts



↓



Financial Statements



\---



Every operational transaction must map to the correct account.



Implementation Consultants often validate these mappings during testing.



Incorrect account mappings produce incorrect financial reports.



\---



\## Consultant Perspective



A successful ERP implementation begins with a well-designed Chart of Accounts.



It is one of the most important configuration activities in any accounting implementation.
