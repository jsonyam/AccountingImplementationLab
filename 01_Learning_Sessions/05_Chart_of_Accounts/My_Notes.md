\# Lesson 05 – My Notes



\## Summary



The Chart of Accounts is the foundation of every accounting system.



It organizes financial information into Assets, Liabilities, Owner's Equity, Revenue, and Expenses.



Every accounting transaction is posted to one or more accounts in the Chart of Accounts.



QuickBooks, Xero, ERP systems, and accounting software all depend on a properly designed COA.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Every business needs a Chart of Accounts.

\- Financial reports depend on correct account mapping.

\- Different businesses customize their Chart of Accounts.

\- Consultants help businesses design and validate the COA.
