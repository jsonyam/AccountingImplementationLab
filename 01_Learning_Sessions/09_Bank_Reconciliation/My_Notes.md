\# Lesson 09 – My Notes



\## Summary



Bank reconciliation compares accounting records with bank statements.



Its goal is to ensure that recorded cash matches actual cash in the bank.



QuickBooks and Xero simplify reconciliation using bank feeds and automatic transaction matching.



Implementation Consultants configure reconciliation settings and help clients establish reliable reconciliation processes.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Reconciliation improves financial accuracy.

\- Bank charges and interest often require accounting adjustments.

\- Outstanding checks and deposits create temporary differences.

\- Bank feeds simplify reconciliation.

\- Reconciliation is a key month-end activity.
