\# Lesson 09 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is bank reconciliation?



It is the process of comparing accounting records with the bank statement to ensure both balances are accurate.



\---



\## 2. Why is reconciliation important?



It identifies errors, missing transactions, bank charges, and other differences.



\---



\## 3. How often should businesses reconcile?



Most businesses perform bank reconciliation monthly, while some reconcile daily or weekly.



\---



\## 4. What is an outstanding check?



A payment recorded by the business that has not yet cleared the bank.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand reconciliation?



Because accurate reconciliation depends on proper banking configuration, transaction matching, and reporting.
