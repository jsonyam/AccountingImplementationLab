\# Lesson 10 – My Notes



\## Summary



Inventory is a current asset that represents products available for sale.



Inventory movements affect both operational processes and accounting records.



QuickBooks, Xero, ERP systems, and Shopify all rely on accurate product and inventory data.



Implementation Consultants configure inventory during system implementation to ensure accurate reporting and smooth operations.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Inventory is an asset.

\- Inventory increases through purchases.

\- Inventory decreases through sales and adjustments.

\- Selling inventory creates Cost of Goods Sold.

\- Product master data is critical to successful implementations.

\- What decreases inventory?

\- What is Cost of Goods Sold?

\- How do ERP systems and accounting systems work together for inventory?
