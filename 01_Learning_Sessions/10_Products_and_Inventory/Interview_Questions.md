\# Lesson 10 – Interview Questions



\## 1. Why is inventory considered an asset?



Because it represents products owned by the business that are expected to generate future revenue.



\---



\## 2. What happens when inventory is sold?



Inventory decreases and Cost of Goods Sold increases.



\---



\## 3. What information belongs in product master data?



SKU, product name, cost, selling price, quantity, supplier, and tax information.



\---



\## 4. Why is inventory accuracy important?



Because inaccurate inventory affects purchasing decisions, customer satisfaction, and financial reporting.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand inventory?



Because inventory configuration affects purchasing, sales, warehouse operations, and accounting.
