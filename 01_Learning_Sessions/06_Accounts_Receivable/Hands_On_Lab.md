\# Lesson 06 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Understand how customer invoices become Accounts Receivable.



\---



\## Business Scenario



You own a premium perfume business.



Customer ABC Boutique purchases:



\- 20 Perfumes

\- Total Invoice = ₱24,000

\- Payment Terms = Net 30



\---



\## Exercise 1



Immediately after creating the invoice:



What increased?



What did not change?



\---



\## Exercise 2



After 30 days the customer pays.



Which account increases?



Which account decreases?



\---



\## Exercise 3



Why is Accounts Receivable considered an Asset?



\---



\## Reflection



What risks exist if customers never pay their invoices?
