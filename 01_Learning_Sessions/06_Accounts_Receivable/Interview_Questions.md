\# Lesson 06 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is Accounts Receivable?



Money customers owe the business for products or services already delivered.



\---



\## 2. Why is AR an Asset?



Because the business expects to collect cash in the future.



\---



\## 3. What happens when a customer pays?



Cash increases.



Accounts Receivable decreases.



\---



\## 4. What report monitors unpaid invoices?



Accounts Receivable Aging Report.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand AR?



Because customer invoicing, payment terms, collections, and financial reporting all depend on proper AR configuration.
