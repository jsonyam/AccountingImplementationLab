\# Lesson 06 – My Notes



\## Summary



Accounts Receivable tracks money customers owe the business.



Customer invoices increase AR.



Customer payments reduce AR.



AR is one of the most important Assets in a business because it represents future cash collections.



QuickBooks, Xero, and ERP systems automate the entire AR process.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- AR is an Asset.

\- Customer invoices create AR.

\- Customer payments reduce AR.

\- AR Aging Reports monitor unpaid invoices.

\- Consultants configure the AR process during implementation.
