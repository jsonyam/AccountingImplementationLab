\# Lesson 13 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is a journal entry?



The accounting record of a financial transaction.



\---



\## 2. Why must journal entries balance?



Because accounting follows double-entry principles.



Total debits must equal total credits.



\---



\## 3. Do users manually create every journal entry?



No.



Most entries are generated automatically by accounting software.



\---



\## 4. When are manual journal entries used?



For adjustments, corrections, accruals, depreciation, and year-end entries.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand journal entries?



Because system configuration determines how business transactions are posted into the General Ledger.
