\# Lesson 13 – My Notes



\## Summary



Journal entries record every accounting transaction.



They follow the rules of double-entry accounting.



QuickBooks, Xero, and ERP systems automatically generate most journal entries from business transactions.



Implementation Consultants configure and validate these posting rules during implementation.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Every transaction becomes a journal entry.

\- Debits must equal credits.

\- Journal entries update the General Ledger.

\- Financial statements are built from journal entries.

\- Consultants validate posting accuracy.
