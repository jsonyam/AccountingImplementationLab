\# Lesson 04 – My Notes



\## Summary



Debits and credits are the foundation of double-entry accounting.



Every transaction affects at least two accounts.



Total debits must always equal total credits.



QuickBooks and Xero automate the accounting entries, but consultants still need to understand the underlying accounting principles.



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Debits do not always mean increase.

\- Credits do not always mean decrease.

\- Assets and Expenses normally increase with debits.

\- Liabilities, Equity, and Revenue normally increase with credits.

\- Every accounting transaction must balance.



\---



\## Questions for Review



\- Which accounts normally increase with a debit?

\- Which accounts normally increase with a credit?

\- Why must debits equal credits?

\- How do QuickBooks and Xero use debits and credits?
