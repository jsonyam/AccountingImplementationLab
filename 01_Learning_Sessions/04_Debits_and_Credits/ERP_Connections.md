\# Lesson 04 – ERP Connections



\## Objective



Understand how ERP systems generate accounting entries automatically.



\---



Business User



↓



Creates Sales Order



↓



ERP Creates Invoice



↓



Accounting Module



↓



Debit Accounts Receivable



Credit Sales Revenue



↓



Financial Statements Updated



\---



Implementation Consultants usually configure business processes.



The accounting entries are generated automatically by the system based on configuration.



Understanding debits and credits helps consultants troubleshoot accounting issues and validate system behavior.



\---



\## Key Takeaway



Business users think in terms of operations.



Accounting systems think in terms of debits and credits.



Implementation Consultants understand both.
