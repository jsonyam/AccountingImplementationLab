\# Lesson 04 – Interview Questions



\## 1. What is a debit?



A debit is one side of an accounting transaction that increases asset and expense accounts while decreasing liability, equity, and revenue accounts.



\---



\## 2. What is a credit?



A credit is one side of an accounting transaction that increases liability, equity, and revenue accounts while decreasing asset and expense accounts.



\---



\## 3. Why are debits and credits important?



They ensure every accounting transaction remains balanced.



\---



\## 4. Do QuickBooks users manually create debits and credits?



Usually no.



QuickBooks automatically creates the accounting entries based on the transaction entered.



\---



\## 5. Why should an Implementation Consultant understand debits and credits?



Because every business process eventually becomes an accounting transaction.
