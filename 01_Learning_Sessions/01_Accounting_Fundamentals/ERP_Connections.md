\# Lesson 01 – ERP Connections



\## Objective



Understand how accounting fits into the complete business process.



\---



\## Business Flow



Supplier



↓



Purchase Inventory



↓



Warehouse Receives Inventory



↓



Products Available for Sale



↓



Customer Places Order



↓



Shopify Creates Order



↓



Payment Received



↓



QuickBooks/Xero Records Financial Transaction



↓



Management Reviews Financial Reports



\---



\## ERP Perspective



ERP manages operational activities:



\- Purchasing

\- Inventory

\- Warehouse

\- Sales

\- Logistics



Accounting software manages:



\- Revenue

\- Expenses

\- Accounts Receivable

\- Accounts Payable

\- Bank Accounts

\- Financial Statements



\---



\## Integration Example



Shopify



↓



QuickBooks Online



↓



ERP



All three systems exchange information to avoid duplicate data entry.



\---



\## Interview Tip



A good Implementation Consultant understands both operational workflows and accounting workflows because every business transaction eventually becomes a financial transaction.
