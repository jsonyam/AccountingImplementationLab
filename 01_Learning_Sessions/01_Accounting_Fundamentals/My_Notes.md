\# Lesson 01 – My Notes



\## Summary



Accounting helps businesses record and understand their financial activities.



Bookkeeping records transactions.



Accounting analyzes those transactions to produce meaningful financial reports.



QuickBooks and Xero are accounting systems.



Shopify is an ecommerce platform.



ERP manages business operations.



A business transaction usually follows this flow:



Customer



↓



Shopify



↓



QuickBooks / Xero



↓



Financial Reports



\---



\## Things I Learned



\- Accounting is the language of business.

\- Every financial transaction should be recorded.

\- Bookkeeping and accounting are different.

\- Shopify does not replace accounting software.

\- ERP and accounting software serve different purposes but work together.

\## Questions for Review



\- Why can't Shopify replace QuickBooks?

\- What is the difference between ERP and accounting software?

\- Why do businesses need financial reports?

\- How do sales become accounting transactions?
