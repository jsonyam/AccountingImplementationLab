\# Lesson 01 – Hands-On Lab



\## Objective



Understand how accounting is used in a real business before using QuickBooks or Xero.



\---



\## Business Scenario



You own a premium perfume store.



Today the following transactions occurred:



1\. Invested ₱100,000 to start the business.

2\. Purchased ₱30,000 worth of perfume inventory.

3\. Sold ₱8,000 worth of perfume.

4\. Customer paid using a credit card.

5\. Paid ₱5,000 for Facebook Ads.

6\. Paid ₱2,000 for internet.
