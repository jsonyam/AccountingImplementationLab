\# Lesson 01 – Interview Questions



\## Basic Questions



\### 1. What is accounting?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



Accounting is the process of recording, organizing, summarizing, and reporting a company's financial transactions so business owners can make informed decisions.



\---



\### 2. What is bookkeeping?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



Bookkeeping is the process of recording daily financial transactions such as sales, purchases, payments, and receipts.



\---



\### 3. What is the difference between bookkeeping and accounting?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



Bookkeeping records financial transactions, while accounting analyzes those transactions and prepares financial reports to support business decisions.



\---



\### 4. Why do businesses use QuickBooks or Xero?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



Businesses use QuickBooks or Xero to record transactions, manage customers and suppliers, reconcile bank accounts, and generate accurate financial reports.



\---



\### 5. Is Shopify an accounting system?



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



No. Shopify is an ecommerce platform. It manages products, customers, and orders, while QuickBooks or Xero manages the financial records created from those transactions.



\---



\## Consultant Question



\### Explain how Shopify, ERP, and QuickBooks work together.



\*\*Sample Answer\*\*



Shopify handles online sales, ERP manages business operations like inventory and purchasing, and QuickBooks records the financial impact of those transactions. Together they create an end-to-end business process from order to financial reporting.
